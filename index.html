The bridge between clean molecule producers and customers

Our mission is to become the trusted bridge between producers and customers of the energy transition—delivering scale, certainty, and value across dislocated markets.

Our Impact

PACC accelerates the commercialization of clean molecules by unlocking new markets, reducing risks to producers, and delivering a reliable supply to customers.

  • Producer‑first mindset and aligned incentives
  • Structured offtake, credit support, and logistics optimization
  • Trust and reliability through disciplined, scalable execution

Our Approach

We de‑risk production, aggregate demand, and create structured pathways for clean molecules.

De‑risk Production

Commercial certainty via structured offtake, credit support, and long‑term agreements.

Aggregate Demand

Create liquidity and price transparency; build scalable customer funnels.

Optimize Logistics

Trailers, storage, pipelines—optimize midstream to lower delivered costs and improve reliability.

Market‑Maker

Act as trusted intermediary to take market risk so producers can focus on operations.

Market Problem

  • Stranded Molecules: Producers lack offtake certainty—limiting investment and scale.
  • Commercial Barriers: Credit requirements, risk exposure, and illiquidity stall deals.
  • High Delivered Costs: Fragmented supply, complex logistics, unreliable delivery.
  • No Market‑Maker: Emerging markets lack a trusted intermediary to connect supply and demand.

PACC Solution

PACC connects producers and customers, turning stranded molecules into scalable markets.

  • Unlock value for producers through commercial certainty
  • De‑risk transactions as a trusted intermediary
  • Lower delivered costs by aggregating volumes and optimizing logistics
  • Create liquidity and price transparency in emerging molecules
