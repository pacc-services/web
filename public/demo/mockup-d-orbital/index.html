Talk to Us How We Work

Our Approach

We de‑risk production, aggregate demand, and create structured pathways for molecules.

  • De‑risk Production — Commercial certainty via structured offtake, credit support, and long‑term agreements.
  • Aggregate Demand — Create liquidity and price transparency; build scalable customer funnels.
  • Optimize Logistics — Trailers, storage, pipelines—optimize midstream to lower delivered costs and improve reliability.
  • Market‑Maker — Act as trusted intermediary to take market risk so producers can focus on operations.

Market Problem

Stranded molecules, commercial barriers, high delivered costs, and no neutral market‑maker.

PACC Solution

PACC connects producers and customers, turning stranded molecules into scalable markets.

  • Unlock value for producers through commercial certainty
  • De‑risk transactions as a trusted intermediary
  • Lower delivered costs by aggregating volumes and optimizing logistics
  • Create liquidity and price transparency in emerging molecules
